regents'​ and chancellor's scholars association (rcsa) is dedicated to helping regents’ and chancellor’s scholarship recipients of uc berkeley develop and grow, hosting numerous events, like faculty game night and alumni dinners, and providing resources, ranging from mentorship programs to resume assistance. The regents’ and chancellor’s scholars title is awarded to the top 2% of undergraduates at uc berkeley.
<br>
During my time in rcsa, I was a part of the media and marketing committee (m&m) and the web-development committee. m&m is responsible for facilitating most avenues of communication between other committees and members of the association. we were able to apply our marketable skills, including knowledge of adobe software (illustrator, indesign, lightroom) and dslr photography, to regularly create promotional material for rcsa. the web-development committee was in charge of maintaing and innovating the rcsa website.
