head of design (2017 - 2018)

hyphen-hacks is an annual twenty-four hour hackathon for high school students across the bay area that takes place at lick-wilmerding high school (lwhs). in 2017, me and a group of high schoolers planned, fundraised, and organized the first student-run hackathon at lwhs. we were able to serve 200+ attendees, partnering with 10+ sponsors.
</p>
