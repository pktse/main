joinery and turning (spring 2019)
<br>
i took this class in the spring semester of my senior year at lick-wilmerding high school. I wanted to take this class because for most of my shop classes, I had used cnc machines for the majority of my project, but in the wood shop, we will primarily use lathes, band saws, table saws, edge sanders, planner, cnc router, laser cutters, 3d printers, plasma cutter, and other tools.
<br>
unlike the other technical arts courses which were more software or cnc focused, I was able to learn more handskills like applying major wood joinery techniques (finger, dove, butt and miter joints) and wood turning.
